Mancala for panel 1

Mancala is a game played with two apponents. The player who takes the most stones, balls or marbles from the other player is the winner. Mancala is a game of strategy. You also may have to risk some of your pieces to get more from your apponent. There are physical and virtual ways to play the game.

Spheres for panel 2

Spheres is a game I came up with while hosting a small party. The games constist of 48 round soft stress balls. The balls are stacked during the game, based on sets of 3. The game is played with three apponents in turn. Questions are asked and whomever gets the correct answer earns a turn to place there sphere. The idea is to get all your balls stacked; without being blocked by the other two players.
